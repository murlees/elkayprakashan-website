About the Author
The heart and pen behind Elkay Prakashan
✍️
Gayathri V
Pen Name: Gayathri Muralidharan
Publisher Author Tamil Poet Storyteller Founder
Founder of Elkay Prakashan, Gayathri is a passionate advocate for Tamil children's literature. Her work bridges India's rich cultural heritage with the needs of today's young readers — through books, reading circles, and community programs.
"Words are seeds — plant them in young hearts and watch a culture bloom."
