Gayathri founded Elkay Prakashan to give Tamil children's literature the home it deserves. Her work bridges India's cultural heritage with the needs of today's young readers — through books, reading circles and community programmes that make reading a lasting habit.

"Words are seeds — plant them in young hearts, and watch a culture bloom."
